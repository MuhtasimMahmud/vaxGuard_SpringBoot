This application helps parents keep track of their children's vaccination schedule conveniently. It allows you to easily manage and monitor the vaccines your child has received and those that are pending.

The application sends you notifications via email or SMS to remind you about upcoming vaccination appointments. You can also view the schedule within the application dashboard.

If you miss a vaccination appointment, the application will automatically reschedule it for you. You'll receive notifications regarding the new appointment date and time.
